Short Bio

MRDJKeremCan (Kerem Can) is an Antalya-based Turkish DJ and electronic music producer, focused on EDM, House and Afro House club music with peak-time energy.

Long Bio

MRDJKeremCan (real name: Kerem Can, born July 22, 1998) is a Turkish DJ and producer from Lara, Antalya. He began his creative journey in 2015 as an animation artist and later shifted into DJing and electronic music production. Known for groove-driven, dancefloor-focused sets across EDM, House and Afro House, he performs as a resident DJ at Inferno Club in Antalya. He also works with Arte Leisure Group as Sound & Light Chief Resident DJ, shaping immersive club experiences through both music and show flow.
