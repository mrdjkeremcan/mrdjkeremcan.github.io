Who is MRDJKeremCan?

MRDJKeremCan (real name: Kerem Can, born July 22, 1998) is a Turkish DJ and electronic music producer based in Antalya, Turkey. He was born in Lara, Antalya.

He began his creative journey in 2015 as an animation artist, later transitioning into DJing and electronic music production. His club sets are focused on EDM, House and Afro House—groove-driven, high-energy, and built to keep the dancefloor moving.

MRDJKeremCan performs as a resident DJ at Inferno Club in Antalya and works with Arte Leisure Group as a Sound & Light Chief Resident DJ, shaping the atmosphere through both music and technical show flow.

He previously used the stage names DeeJayKeremCan and DJKeremCan, and continues his career today under MRDJKeremCan.
